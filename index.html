About Me

Hello, Salam, Konnichiwa, Namaste to everyone! My name is Nisma Nawaz. I was born in Pakistan and raised in Kuwait. Currently, I am a student at Concordia University in Montreal,pursuing a Joint Major in Computation Arts and Computer Science. I have always been fascinated by technology and its boundless creative potential. Over the years,I've explored various mediums and continue to learn about new and exciting advancements in the field. My journey is driven by a passion for innovation and a desire to push the boundaries of what's possible. Feel free to explore my portfolio to see how I blend creativity with technology!
